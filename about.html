About this map

This map tracks fountains and memorials in Washington, D.C. that are being restored, rehabilitated, or upgraded as part of the federal beautification initiative ahead of America's 250th anniversary in 2026.

Every location on this map is sourced directly from official government records. No location is included unless it appears in an NPS press release or a separately documented federal project.
